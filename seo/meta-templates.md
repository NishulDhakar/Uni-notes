# SEO Meta Templates

## 1. Title Tags

### Homepage
**Template:** `RGPV Notes, PYQs & Syllabus (B.Tech, Diploma, MCA) | RGPVNotes.in`
**Length:** ~55-60 chars

### Course/Branch Page (e.g., B.Tech CSE)
**Template:** `RGPV {Branch} Notes & Syllabus ({Semester} Sem) | RGPVNotes.in`
**Example:** `RGPV CSE Notes & Syllabus (3rd Sem) | RGPVNotes.in`

### Subject Page (e.g., Machine Learning)
**Template:** `{Subject} Notes RGPV {Branch} {Semester} Sem | Download PDF`
**Example:** `Machine Learning Notes RGPV CSE 7th Sem | Download PDF`

### Unit Page (e.g., Unit 1)
**Template:** `{Subject} Unit {UnitNumber}: {UnitName} Notes RGPV | Free PDF`
**Example:** `Machine Learning Unit 1: Supervised Learning Notes RGPV | Free PDF`

### PYQ Page
**Template:** `RGPV {Subject} Previous Year Question Papers (PYQ) | Download PDF`
**Example:** `RGPV Data Structures Previous Year Question Papers (PYQ) | Download PDF`
